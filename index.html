About us

THE VALUE OF MODERN ENGLISH ACADEMY

Modern English Academy nestled in a 2 acres campus in the cantonment area of Barrackpore is an English medium co-ed school; with higher secondary classes .

The school was founded to fulfill the need for a secondary school for the children who completed their education in Modern School, Barrackpore.

HISTORY OF MODERN ENGLISH ACADEMY

The school was started in April 1998 by the Barrackpore Park Road Christian Education Society - a Society registered under the W.B. Societies' Act. Mrs. Enid Isaac, an educationist with over 50 years experience is the founder.

AWARDS AND RECOGNITION

School is winner of The Telegraph School Awards For Excellence- The IIHM Award for Excellence in Academics (ICSE and ISC) Certificate Of Merit 2017 British Council International School Assessment Award 2014-2017
